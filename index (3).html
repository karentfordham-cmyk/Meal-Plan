Meals
M-A Tofu Tikka Masala (spinach & cauliflower)
350 kcal · 34g protein
▾
Ingredients (per serving)
  • firm tofu, calcium-set (g): 170 g
  • spinach (g): 60 g
  • cauliflower florets (g): 200 g
  • tomato passata (g): 120 g
  • Greek yogurt 0% (g): 100 g
  • garlic cloves: 2
  • ginger (g): 10 g
  • turmeric (tsp): 0.5 tsp
  • garam masala (tsp): 1 tsp
  • smoked paprika (tsp): 0.5 tsp
  • chili flakes: 1
  • lemon juice (tbsp): 1 tbsp
Method / flavour notes
1) Press tofu 5–10 min; cube.
2) Sauté garlic + ginger with spices (turmeric, garam masala, paprika) in a non‑stick pan (add water if needed).
3) Add passata; simmer 5 min. Stir in tofu + cauliflower; cook until tender.
4) Stir in spinach to wilt; remove from heat and fold through yogurt + lemon.
Flavour boosters: extra garam masala, chilli, lemon, pinch of salt.
M-B Seitan Garlic-Ginger Broccoli Stir Fry + Cauli Rice
295 kcal · 37g protein
▾
Ingredients (per serving)
  • seitan strips (g): 120 g
  • broccoli florets (g): 150 g
  • cabbage (g): 120 g
  • cauliflower rice (g): 200 g
  • garlic cloves: 2
  • ginger (g): 8 g
  • soy sauce/tamari (tbsp): 1.5 tbsp
  • rice vinegar (tbsp): 1 tbsp
  • lime juice (tbsp): 1 tbsp
  • extra virgin olive oil (tsp): 0.5 tsp
Method / flavour notes
1) Steam/microwave cauliflower rice until hot.
2) Stir‑fry garlic + ginger, then seitan, broccoli, cabbage.
3) Add soy sauce, vinegar, lime; finish with ½ tsp olive oil.
Flavour boosters: chilli flakes, black pepper, extra lime, toasted sunflower seeds (optional 5 g).
M-C Tempeh Cashew-Lime 'Satay' + Veg
360 kcal · 30g protein
▾
Ingredients (per serving)
  • tempeh (g): 100 g
  • broccoli florets (g): 150 g
  • cauliflower florets (g): 200 g
  • cashews (g): 10 g
  • garlic cloves: 1
  • lime juice (tbsp): 1.5 tbsp
  • soy sauce/tamari (tbsp): 1 tbsp
  • curry powder (tsp): 1 tsp
  • chili flakes: 1
  • extra virgin olive oil (tsp): 0.5 tsp
Method / flavour notes
1) Steam broccoli + cauliflower.
2) Pan‑sear tempeh; add garlic + curry powder + chilli.
3) Blend/whisk cashews with lime + soy sauce + water to make a bright sauce; toss through.
Flavour boosters: extra lime, curry powder, pinch of salt.
M-D TVP & Lentil-Shiitake Ragù over Zucchini Noodles
330 kcal · 35g protein
▾
Ingredients (per serving)
  • TVP (textured veg protein), dry (g): 25 g
  • lentils, cooked (g): 80 g
  • shiitake mushrooms (g): 120 g
  • tomato passata (g): 150 g
  • zucchini noodles (g): 250 g
  • spinach (g): 50 g
  • garlic cloves: 2
  • oregano/basil: 1
  • extra virgin olive oil (tsp): 0.5 tsp
Method / flavour notes
1) Rehydrate TVP in hot water with a pinch of salt.
2) Sauté garlic + shiitake; add passata + herbs and simmer.
3) Add lentils + TVP; reduce until thick.
4) Toss with zucchini noodles; fold in spinach to wilt.
Flavour boosters: chilli flakes, black pepper, splash of vinegar.
M-E Lemon-Herb Tofu + Asparagus & Kale
320 kcal · 36g protein
▾
Ingredients (per serving)
  • firm tofu, calcium-set (g): 200 g
  • asparagus (g): 150 g
  • kale (g): 80 g
  • Greek yogurt 0% (g): 80 g
  • garlic cloves: 1
  • lemon juice (tbsp): 1.5 tbsp
  • dijon mustard (tsp): 1 tsp
  • dill/parsley: 1
  • extra virgin olive oil (tsp): 0.5 tsp
Method / flavour notes
1) Marinate tofu 10 min in lemon + mustard + garlic + herbs.
2) Grill/pan‑sear tofu; roast or sauté asparagus.
3) Massage kale with lemon + a pinch of salt; add yogurt dressing.
Flavour boosters: extra lemon zest, dill/parsley, cracked pepper.
M-F Beetroot-Chickpea-Quinoa Salad + Tofu
370 kcal · 32g protein
▾
Ingredients (per serving)
  • firm tofu, calcium-set (g): 170 g
  • beetroot, cooked (g): 120 g
  • chickpeas, cooked (g): 50 g
  • quinoa, cooked (g): 60 g
  • spinach (g): 50 g
  • cabbage (g): 80 g
  • Greek yogurt 0% (g): 80 g
  • orange or lemon juice (tbsp): 1.5 tbsp
  • garlic cloves: 1
  • extra virgin olive oil (tsp): 0.5 tsp
  • walnuts (g): 5 g
Method / flavour notes
1) Crisp tofu in a non‑stick pan; season.
2) Toss beetroot, chickpeas, quinoa, spinach, cabbage.
3) Mix yogurt + citrus + garlic + ½ tsp olive oil; drizzle.
4) Top with 5 g walnuts.
Flavour boosters: orange zest, vinegar, pinch of salt.
M-G Miso Shiitake Soup with Tofu + Edamame
360 kcal · 33g protein
▾
Ingredients (per serving)
  • firm tofu, calcium-set (g): 200 g
  • edamame (soy beans), shelled (g): 80 g
  • shiitake mushrooms (g): 100 g
  • cabbage (g): 120 g
  • spinach (g): 60 g
  • miso paste (g): 15 g
  • garlic cloves: 1
  • ginger (g): 6 g
  • rice vinegar (tbsp): 1 tbsp
Method / flavour notes
1) Simmer mushrooms, cabbage, ginger, garlic in broth 5–7 min.
2) Add tofu + edamame; warm through.
3) Off heat, dissolve miso in a little hot broth then stir back in.
Flavour boosters: rice vinegar, chilli, extra ginger.
